Spicy Chorizo Tomato Spaghetti



Another excellent Spicy Cherry Tomato Spaghetti" recipe

This is another favourite Spicy Tomato Spaghetti recipe of mine. This one contains chorizo and chilli, uses 9 ingredients, and can put together in just 15 mins

Ingredients for 2

  • 1 lemon
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 10g parsley
  • 250g baby plum tomatoes
  • 1/2 tsp of dried chilli flakes
  • 100g diced chorizo
  • 1 vegetable stock cube
  • 200g spaghetti
  • 30g Italian hard cheese(grated)

Here's the steps for the recipe.

  1. Boil a kettle, and preheat the grill to high
  2. Cut half of the baby plum tomatoes in half. Add the halved cherry tomatoes and remaining whole ones, to a baking tray with a drizzle of olive oil and a pinch of salt. Give everything a good mix and put under grill
  3. Whilst the tomatoes are cooking, add the spaghetti to pot of boiled water with a pinch of salt. Bring to the boil over a high heat
  4. Once done, drain the cooked spaghetti and reserve a cup of the starchy pasta water
  5. Meanwhile, heat a large pan over a medium - high heat. Once hot, add the diced chorizo and chilli flakes(use less if you can't handle the heat) and cook for 3 mins until beginning to crisp
  6. Peel and finely slice the garlic cloves, and add to the pan with 2 tbsp of olive oil. Cook for 1 minute
  7. Remove the pan from the heat and add 100ml of the reserved starchy pasta water, then return the pan to heat and crumble up the vegetable stock cube and parsley, and add it to the pan. Give everything a good stir
  8. Add the drained spaghetti to the pan, with the juice of half the lemon. Add a splash of more of the starchy pasta water if your sauce is looking a bit dry
  9. Serve the spicy chorizo spagetti, topped with the grilled baby plum tomatoes, and garnish with Italian hard cheese
