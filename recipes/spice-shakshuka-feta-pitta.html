Spiced Shakshuka With Feta And Crunchy Pitta

Home

Try this spicy tomato-and-egg-dish known as
"Shaksuka"

This healthy Spiced shakshuka recipe is sure to be a favourite in your household.
This one contains eggs in tomato sauce with pepper, onion and spices, and served with crispy toasted pitta and feta cheese.
It's a quick one to knock up (20 mins)

Ingredients for 2

  • 1 red onion
  • 1 red pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper
  • 15g pumpkin seeds
  • 80g spinach
  • 30g feta
  • 210g canned chopped tomatoes
  • 5.5g vegetable stock cube
  • 2 wholemeal pittas

Here's the steps for the recipe.

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees celsius, and boil half a kettle
  2. Peel and finely slice the red onion. Deseed the red pepper, and cut into thin strips
  3. Heat a large pan with a splash of olive oil over a medium heat. Once hot add the sliced onion and pepper strips with a pinch of salt and cook for 5-7 mins until soft
  4. Meanwhile, peel and finely slice the garlic
  5. Dissolve the vegetable stock cube in 100ml of boiled water.
  6. When the pepper and onion have softened, add the chopped garlic, smoked paprika, ground cumin and the cayenne pepper (use less if you can't handle the heat), and cook for 1-2 mins until fragrant
  7. Once fragrant, add the can of chopped tomatoes, and the dissolved vegetable stock to the pan. Increase the heat to high and bring to the boil. Once boiling, reduce heat to medium and cook for 6-8 mins
  8. While the sauce is cooking, cut the wholemeal pittas into triangles, and add them to a baking tray. Put the tray in the oven for 8 mins. keep an eye on them to make sure they don't burn!
  9. When the sauce has thickened, add the spinach, and cook until wilted. Once wilted, crack the eggs over the sauce, and cover for 4-5 mins or until the eggs are cooked through
  10. "Serve the spiced shakshuka with toasted pitta triangles to the side. Garnish with pumpkin seeds and a pinch of black pepper. Crumble over the feta and tuck in!
