Spicy Tomato Spaghetti



My favourite "Spicy Cherry Tomato Spaghetti" recipe

This Spicy Cherry Tomato Spaghetti recipe goes down so well in our household. It's quick and easy to make(10 min), uses only 7 ingredients, and is absolutely delicious.

Ingredients for 2

  • 250g cherry tomatoes
  • 80g spinach
  • 30g tomato paste
  • 15g garlic paste
  • 180g spagetti
  • 40g 'Nduja
  • 30g Italian hard cheese

Here's the steps for the recipe.

  1. Boil a kettle, and heat a large pan with generous amount of olive oil over a medium - high heat
  2. Cut the cherry tomatoes in half. Once the pan is hot, add the halved cherry tomatoes to the pan with a pinch of salt
  3. Cook for the tomatoes for 2-3 min or until they begin to break down. Once broken down add the garlic paste and a pinch of sugar. Cook for 1-2 min until fragrant
  4. Add the spaghetti to a pot of boiled water with a large pinch of salt and bring to the boil over a high heat. Cook the spagetti until it's "Al dente"
  5. Once cooked, drain and reserve a cup of the starchy pasta water. Return the drained spaghetti to the pot with a drizzle of olive oil and set aside
  6. Wash the spinach , and pat dry with kitchen paper
  7. Once the tomatoes are fragrant, add the tomato paste and spinach, and cook for 2-3 min or until the spinach has slightly wilted
  8. When done, add the 'Nduja (use less if you can't handle the heat) to the pan and add a splash of the reserved pasta water. Cook for 2-3 min until thickened
  9. Once thickened, serve the Spicy cherry tomato sauce over the spaghetti
  10. Sprinkle over the Italian hard cheese and serve
